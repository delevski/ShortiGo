Privacy Policy

Effective June 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ShortiGo processes information when you use the ShortiGo mobile application and related services.

Information we process

  • Account information, including your email address, display name, authentication identifier, and sign-in provider.
  • App activity, including saved series, viewing activity, reward balances, subscription status, and transaction records.
  • Device, diagnostics, performance, and crash information used to keep the app reliable and secure.
  • Advertising and purchase information processed by our advertising and subscription providers.

How we use information

  • Provide authentication and personalize your app experience.
  • Maintain My List, rewards, and subscription access.
  • Deliver rewarded advertisements and process purchases.
  • Detect abuse, prevent fraud, troubleshoot, and improve performance.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our policies.

Service providers

ShortiGo uses service providers that process information under their own terms and privacy policies. These may include Firebase for authentication, database, performance, and hosting services; Google AdMob for advertising; RevenueCat and the applicable app store for subscriptions; Sentry for crash reporting; and Cloudinary for media delivery.

Data retention

Personal profile data, saved content, and viewing activity are deleted when you delete your account. Transaction records may be retained after account deletion where reasonably necessary for fraud prevention, security, accounting, dispute resolution, and legal compliance.

Your choices

You can delete your ShortiGo account from the app. See the account-deletion instructions. You can also manage advertising and purchase settings through your device and app-store settings.

Children

ShortiGo is not directed to children under 13, or the minimum age required in their country to consent to data processing.

Security and international processing

We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect information. Information may be processed in countries other than your own wherever our service providers operate.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as ShortiGo changes. The effective date above identifies the latest version.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact ShortiGo through the support contact shown on the ShortiGo listing in the Apple App Store or Google Play.
