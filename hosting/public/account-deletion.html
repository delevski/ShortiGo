Privacy Policy Account Deletion

Delete your ShortiGo account

Updated June 4, 2026

You can permanently delete your account inside ShortiGo.

Deletion steps

  1. Open ShortiGo and sign in to the account you want to delete.
  2. Open the Profile tab.
  3. Find Account & Subscription.
  4. Tap Delete account.
  5. Review the confirmation message and confirm deletion.

Data deleted

Account deletion removes your Firebase Authentication account, ShortiGo profile, saved series, and viewing activity.

Data retained

Transaction records may be retained where reasonably necessary for fraud prevention, security, accounting, dispute resolution, and legal compliance. Retained records are protected from normal app access.

Need help?

If you cannot access the app, contact ShortiGo through the support contact shown on the ShortiGo listing in the Apple App Store or Google Play.
